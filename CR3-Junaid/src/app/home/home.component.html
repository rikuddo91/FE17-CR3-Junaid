Ristorante Il Gambero Rosso

Serving you for generations.

A Cozy & Comfortable Place With Authentic Italian Classics

Gambero Rosso is a family style restaurant offering exceptional value to its guests through the many dishes of Southern Italian cuisine. Gambero Rosso was established with a singular vision; bringing his guests “wow-factor” sized portions and making any meal feel like an authentic Italian experience.

As we have nothing to hide, nor we want to lock away our chefs, our kitchen is designed in a way you can have a look on meal prepartion, get in touch with our cooks, or just to perceive it as a background component of our ambience.

We offer the perfect place for various occasions such as:

  • cerimonies like weddings
  • candle-light dinner for two
  • Birthdays

We like to point out that we see it as our duty to make your time at our place unique and enjoyable. The more we know about your needs, the better we can satisfy them. Please don't hesitate to inform as at any time.

Why choose us

What makes us unique?

  1. Classic Dishes with unqiue twist

    We only use high quality ingredients produced in the region by people we know, trying to conserve their original taste throughout the cooking process and not forgetting about the little "secrets" of traditional italian cuisine.

  2. Cozy atmosphere

    We position ourselves in the middle of androgyn style, freezing gourmet temples and crowded, popular restaurants. Our wish is to create a cosy atmosphere without overstepping the limits of other customers well-being.

  3. Catering to your personal needs

    Do you need a gluten-free menu or are you suffering from other food-allergies like tree nuts, cow's milk or wheat. Just let us know, we can manage that!
